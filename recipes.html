Katorade Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 2 cups chopped kale
  • 1/2 cup soy milk
  • 1 tbsp flax seeds
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • 1 sprinkle fun ;)

Instructions

  1. Place the banana, kale, soy milk, flax seeds, and maple syrup into a blender. Cover, and puree until smooth. Serve over ice.
